University of Tomorrow

Academic Commitment

UPES has established itself as a leader in providing education that aligns with the dynamic needs of the energy and petroleum sectors. The university is committed to nurturing skilled professionals who can contribute significantly to these industries and related domains.

Specialized Programs

UPES is renowned for its specialized programs that cover a wide spectrum of disciplines, including engineering, management, law, design, and more. The curriculum is carefully crafted to meet industry standards, ensuring that students receive practical, industry-relevant knowledge.

State Of The Art-Facilities

The campuses at Bidholi and Kandholi are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and cutting-edge research centers. These facilities are designed to provide students with hands-on experience and exposure to the latest technologies in their respective fields.

Immersive Learninig Environment

The tranquil and quiet surroundings of the campuses create an environment conducive to focused learning and academic growth. Students at UPES not only benefit from traditional classroom teaching but also engage in experiential learning, projects, and industry interactions.

Alumni Success Stories:

The success stories of UPES alumni reflect the effectiveness of the education they received. Many graduates have gone on to make significant contributions to their industries, showcasing the practical applicability of their education at UPES.In essence, the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies emerges as not just an educational institution but a dynamic hub for learning, innovation, and growth, preparing its students to become leaders and experts in the energy and petroleum sectors. The idyllic campuses and comprehensive educational approach contribute to an enriching academic journey for every student at UPES.
